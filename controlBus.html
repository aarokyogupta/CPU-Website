Control Bus

The control bus is a type of bus that is used to transfer control signals between the CPU and other devices in the computer system. The control bus is used to transmit signals that control the operations of the CPU, memory, and other parts of a computer. The control bus is used to transfer signals such as read, write, and interrupt signals between the CPU and other devices. The control bus is an important part of the computer system and is used to ensure that the different components of the system can communicate with each other.
